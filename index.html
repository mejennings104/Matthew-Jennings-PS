About Me

Why tech?

I have always had an interest in technology after being surrounded by it my entire life. From gameboys, to televisions, the beginning of the internet, the rapid succession of technological advancement has intrigued me as I have always wondered exactly how technology works. I enjoy figuring out puzzles, solving math equations, and tinkering with single-board computers. Learning coding languages seemed like it would be a good fit and I hope to advance this skill.

My Hobbies and Personal Details
